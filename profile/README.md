Efflexion helps organisations stabilise fragile spreadsheets, automate repetitive workflows, and build reporting that supports confident day-to-day decisions.

Many businesses rely on spreadsheets as operational systems.  
Over time those tools often become slow, fragile, or difficult to trust.

Efflexion restores structure and reliability so those systems support the business instead of slowing it down.

---

# What Efflexion focuses on

## Spreadsheet Stabilisation

Rescuing spreadsheets that have become fragile or difficult to maintain.

Typical improvements include:

- safer formulas and clearer structure  
- reduced operational risk  
- easier handover between team members  
- spreadsheets that remain stable as data grows  

---

## Workflow Automation

Removing repetitive manual processes using Excel and Power Query.

Common results:

- fewer copy-paste steps  
- faster reporting cycles  
- consistent data processing  
- reduced human error  

---

## Business Reporting

Turning operational data into clear, decision-ready reporting using Excel or Power BI.

Typical outcomes:

- consolidated operational visibility  
- consistent reporting structure  
- reduced manual report preparation  

---

# How engagements usually begin

Most work starts with a **Spreadsheet Health Audit**.

This short diagnostic identifies:

- structural risks  
- fragile formulas  
- workflow inefficiencies  
- opportunities for automation  

The audit provides a clear scope before any corrective work begins.
